Try It Yourself: Text Segmentation Challenge

Place line breaks between sentences to create semantically coherent chunks.

How It Works:

  • Click the dividing line between sentences to add a chunk break
  • Click again to remove a break
  • Sentences between breaks are automatically grouped into colored chunks
  • When ready, click "Compare with Model" to see how your chunking compares to the model's segmentation
  • Click "Reset" to start over
